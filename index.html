An Introduction

As a Leadership and Team Development Coach, I partner with organizations to help improve team dynamics and empower leadership skills at every level. I trained in Energy Leadership Coaching with the Institute of Professional Excellence in Coaching (iPEC), a meticulous and certified program. I also hold an Associates coaching certification within the International Coaching Federation.


My 31 years’ experience in the transportation and logistics industry with UPS, 23 of those at the senior level, allows me to blend knowledge and analytics with my coaching, providing you with objective evidence showing the return on investment (ROI) in my coaching services.


I enjoy the outdoors with my wife Rea, in a variety of ways, kayaking, hiking, and in winter a snowshoe trek or two. The Kansas City area has been our home off and on over the last 30 years, and we love everything it has to offer.


The desired approach to leadership is defined as how you interact with everyone, including yourself. Everyone is a leader either by choice or by default. The question is not whether or not we are leaders, but how well do we lead?

Blogs & Books

Blogs:


I enjoy writing on the topic of Leadership and how we may present leadership in ourselves and to others in life and at work. The idea behind my blogs and newsletters is to provide some mental prompts to stir up my reader's self-awareness and promote personal growth. These thoughts are spontaneous, and some come from my interactions with my clients, other professionals and some, well, they are my own personal musings.

So then, I've decided to share what I have found purposeful and relevant. Some of you receiving this are familiar with the topic and some, well, I'm hoping to spur your curiosity to experience something new. To follow my writings you can subscribe to my blog and sign up for my newsletter.
